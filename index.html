How to apply

  1. Shake well before use.
  2. Apply a small drop inside the jaw, on the fitting, or both.
  3. Operate the tool as normal. Reapply as needed.
Tip: Start with less than you think. You can always add a tiny bit more.

FAQ

Is it oily or wet?

It’s a dry lubricant. The goal is improved movement without a greasy residue.

How much should I use?

Use a small drop inside the jaw, on the fitting, or both. Reapply as needed.

Do I need to clean the jaws first?

If jaws are heavily contaminated, a quick clean can help. Then apply a small amount of Press Mate Pro.
